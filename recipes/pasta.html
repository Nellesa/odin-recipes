Pasta

Italian pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine, known for its simplicity and delicious flavors. The most basic Italian pasta dish involves cooking pasta until it's al dente and then tossing it with a flavorful sauce. Common pasta types include spaghetti, fettuccine, penne, or any pasta of your choice. The sauce can vary from a simple tomato and basil sauce to more complex options like carbonara or Alfredo. Italian pasta is all about balancing the flavors of the sauce with the texture of perfectly cooked pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Sauce of your choice
  • Optional toppings like grated
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper for seasoning

How to Make Pasta Step-By-Step

  1. Boil the Water
  2. Add the Pasta
  3. Prepare the Sauce
  4. Toss the Pasta
  5. Combine and Season
  6. Serve and Garnish
