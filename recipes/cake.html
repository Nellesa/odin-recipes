Cake

Cake is a delightful and versatile dessert loved for its sweet and tender crumb. This general cake recipe can serve as a base for various cake variations, from classic vanilla to chocolate, and it's perfect for celebrating special occasions or simply satisfying your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (2 sticks)
  • softened 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • extract 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
